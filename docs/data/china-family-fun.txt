北京，是一座既古老又充满活力的超级都市，超过三千年建城史、八百六十余年建都史的历
史文化名城。是全球拥有世界文化遗产数量最多的城市之一。北京融合了厚重的历史与现代
的活力，历史上曾是辽、金、元、明、清五个朝代的都城，恢弘的紫禁城、壮丽的长城、典
雅的皇家园林，无不诉说着东方文明的辉煌与智慧。这座城市既保留着胡同、四合院的传统
肌理，也矗立着国家体育场（鸟巢）、中央电视台总部大楼等现代建筑奇观，完美展现了古
老文明与现代发展的对话与交融。
我们特别为家庭旅行设计了轻松有趣的节奏，北京的行程将融合历史文化、胡同故事和亲子
互动，是一次充满发现和惊喜的体验。

Arrive at Beijing Capital International Airport, after a dedicated airport transfer, check in at the
hotel.
Beijing is an ancient yet vibrant super metropolis, a historic and cultural city with over three
thousand years of urban history and more than eight hundred and sixty years as a capital. It is one
of the cities in the world with the largest number of UNESCO World Heritage sites. Beijing blends
profound history with modern energy. It served as the capital for five dynasties in history—Liao, Jin,
Yuan, Ming, and Qing. The majestic Forbidden City, the magnificent Great Wall, and the elegant
imperial gardens all narrate the brilliance and wisdom of Eastern civilization. The city preserves the
traditional fabric of hutongs and courtyard residences, while also standing alongside modern
architectural wonders such as the National Stadium (Bird’s Nest) and the CCTV Headquarters,
perfectly illustrating the dialogue and integration between ancient civilization and modern
development.
We have specially designed a relaxed and fun pace for family travel. The Beijing itinerary will
combine historical culture, hutong stories, and parent-child interaction, offering an experience full
of discovery and surprise.

上午（文化核心）：【故宫博物院】
故宫，明清两代皇家宫殿，世界现存最大木构建筑群。沿中轴线穿越太和殿、乾清宫至御花
园，见证中国礼制秩序与建筑美学巅峰。近万间殿宇藏有百万珍宝，每片琉璃瓦都在诉说六
百年风云。这里不仅是皇宫，更是读懂东方文明的密钥。
Morning (Cultural Core): [The Forbidden City]
The Forbidden City, the imperial palace of the Ming and Qing dynasties, is the world's largest
surviving complex of wooden structures. Walking along the central axis from the Hall of Supreme
Harmony and the Palace of Heavenly Purity to the Imperial Garden, visitors witness the pinnacle of
Chinese ritual architecture and aesthetic design. With nearly ten thousand halls housing millions of
treasures, every glazed tile whispers stories of six centuries of history. This is not merely a royal
residence—it is the key to understanding Eastern civilization.

Afternoon (Hutongs & Prince's Mansion): [Shichahai] + [Prince Gong’s Mansion]
Shichahai, the soulful water network of old Beijing, consists of Qianhai, Houhai, and Xihai lakes. It
preserves the most intact layout of hutongs and the vibrant atmosphere of local life. Visitors can
take a rickshaw ride through the blue-brick and grey-tiled alleyways while listening to the driver's
tales of old Beijing. In summer, you can enjoy lotus flowers on a boat, while in winter, the lakes turn
into lively ice-skating rinks. Along the shores, modern bars mingle with century-old traditional
shops, making it a "living museum of Beijing where past and present blend seamlessly."

上午（历史奇观）：【八达岭长城】
八达岭长城是明长城最具代表性的段落，以雄伟险峻、保存完整著称。登临“好汉坡”可俯
瞰群山间巨龙蜿蜒的壮阔景象，瓮城、敌台等防御体系展现了古代军事智慧。
Morning (Historical Wonder): [Badaling Great Wall]
The Badaling section is the most iconic and best-preserved part of the Ming Dynasty Great Wall,
renowned for its majestic and precipitous terrain. Ascending the "Hero's Slope" offers a
breathtaking panoramic view of the winding "stone dragon" traversing the mountain ranges. Its
sophisticated defense system, including the barbican ("Wengcheng") and watchtowers, vividly
showcases the ancient military ingenuity of China.

Xi'an, formerly known as Chang'an, is one of China's Four Great Ancient Capitals and was

this system. It’s not only a lesson in engineering but also an enlightening introduction to Eastern
wisdom.

The Subtle Thread of Family Growth
For the 5-year-old: A sensory awakening—from touching the clay of a terracotta warrior to
stroking the lake stones in a Suzhou garden, tiny hands collected textures that form their earliest
memories of China.
For the 14-year-old: An intellectual leap—comparing the axial symmetry of the Forbidden City
with the spatial logic of Swiss cathedrals, analyzing the engineering philosophy behind Dujiangyan
and Alpine tunnels, sparking the seeds of cross-cultural critical thinking.
The Core Values of Our Service Design
A Rhythmic Pace: Alternating between the historical depth of Beijing and Xi’an, and the living
aesthetics of Chengdu and Jiangnan.
Multidimensional Experiences: Each city blended “museum insight + hands-on heritage +
nature immersion.”
Cultural Translation: Throughout the journey, we used comparative narration (e.g., the Great
Wall vs. Swiss mountain defense systems).
