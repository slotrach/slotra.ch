→ LLMs are great at math...or are they? LLMs have done well on tasks with concrete answers, but what about proofs—the central tool of mathematics? 

→ We show SOTA LLMs fail at generating math proofs, scoring < 5% on avg. on the 2025 USA High-School Math Olympiad (USAMO) that took place few days ago.

→ For our comprehensive evaluation we asked expert human graders to assess solutions from LLMs such as o3-mini, Claude 3.7, and Deepseek-R1.

→ Our analysis also uncovered common issues, including logical errors, unjustified assumptions, and artifacts from reinforcement learning methods (like GRPO) that cause models to favor neatly formatted final answers—even when this just added confusion.
